About me

I'm Ben, a developer who came to code through storytelling. After years in videojournalism and digital marketing, I discovered that building things for the web let me combine creative problem-solving with technical craft. Originally from France, I’m now based in the United States.

I'm a builder at heart — I learn best with my hands on the keyboard, creating everything from simple sites to dynamic applications. Right now I'm deep in JavaScript and APIs, and gearing up to explore React and Next.js.

Outside of dev work, you'll find me taking care of my two kids or at the dojo practicing Kendo.
